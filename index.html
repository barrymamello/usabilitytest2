Usability Study

Methods

  1. Buy products and goods
  2. Compare products
  3. Compare prices

Results

  1. Subject was able to purchase goods and products, compare products, and compare prices
  2. It was easily accessible
  3. It took less than 2 minutes for the test subject to do the representative tasks

Discussions

  1. We learnt that usability of a website is important because it enhances the user's experience and it allows the flow of tasks on the website.
  2. The current design is effective as it allows the user to utilise a number of functions on the website that are useful to them.
  3. Their website's usability is working pretty well, there are no significant changes that need to be made to the website
